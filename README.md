- Business Insights 360 – Power BI Dashboard

Business Insights 360 is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to provide a 360° view of business performance across finance, sales, marketing, supply chain, and executive metrics.
The dashboard enables stakeholders to analyze key business KPIs, monitor trends, and make data-driven decisions through dynamic visualizations.

- Project Overview

This project focuses on transforming raw business data into actionable insights using Power BI. It integrates multiple business domains such as finance, product performance, regional sales, and operational metrics into a single unified dashboard.

Users can explore data through filters like region, market, customer, segment, and time (year/quarter) to analyze performance across different dimensions.

- Dashboard Modules
1. Finance View
   * Profit & Loss statement analysis
   * Net Sales, Gross Margin %, and Net Profit % KPIs
   * Financial comparison against benchmarks
   * Time-based sales performance trends

2.Sales View
  * Product and customer sales analysis
  * Profitability and growth matrix visualization
  * Sales performance by region and market

3.Marketing View
  * Product-level performance tracking
  * Net sales, gross margin, and profit metrics
  * Performance matrix for product categories

4.Supply Chain View
  * Forecast accuracy tracking
  * Risk and error analysis across segments and products

5.Executive View
  * High-level overview for decision makers
  * Consolidated KPIs across all business dimensions

- Key Insights Provided
  * Net Sales performance over time
  * Product category profitability
  * Regional market performance
  * Unit economics (COGS vs Gross Margin)
  * Top and bottom performing products and customers

- Tools & Technologies
  * Power BI Desktop
  * Power Query for data transformation
  * DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for calculations
  * Interactive Visualizations (Matrix, Charts, KPIs, Filters)

- Features
  * Interactive slicers (Region, Market, Customer, Segment)
  * Time analysis (Year, Quarter, YTD)
  * Profit & Loss financial breakdown
  * Performance matrix for product categories
  * Regional and customer-level insights
  * Executive-level business overview

- Objective
  * To create a comprehensive business intelligence dashboard that helps organizations:
    - Monitor financial and operational performance
    - Identify profitable products and markets
    - Track business growth trends
    - Support strategic decision making
